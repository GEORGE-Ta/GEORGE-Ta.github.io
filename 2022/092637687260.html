Thoughts

I told myself to write my diary in English.
However, it’s hard to express my thoughts in English.
I need to think more than just jot it down.
It’s true though.
However, if I do not do any practice.
My English will never be better.
And using English to write could help me to think in English.
In conclusion, taking notes in English is blood earned.

Today, I took the Master’s Lecture.
Our professor is 王品先院士.
His lecture is much better than the one last week.

The last one is just like reading PPT.
However, Academician Wang’s lecture is more like that he talking about what he really wants to say to the young.
However, I don’t know if I’m too sensitive or if I’m right.
I felt his disdain for our school.
It’s like he does not think the students in our school could actually do something amazing.
I do not blame him, but I still feel bad about that.
I had to get to a better school for my graduate study.

Then, let’s talk about the lecture itself.
The academician talked about culture and science.
The main idea I got from the lecture is that:
WE CHINESE
are specific in the world.
However, it’s hard for us to study science in Chinese.
There is too much literature written in English.
Studying English is our only choice.
But, we have our own advantages.
Our culture is so inclusive, and peaceful.
We take peace as truth.
We do not have slave times.
We do not even have slaves.

We could use our advantage.
However, we need to find a way to use it.

I want to talk about I am the world.
However, I truly could not talk about it in English.
And I truly need to do some records when I read SA or anything else in English.

One most important thing is that:
We need to think in our own minds,
we could not do everything for our boss,
It’s better to like what we like, but what we could make money.

求之不得，寤寐思复。悠哉游哉，辗转反侧。
参差荇菜，左右采之。窈窕淑女，琴瑟友之。
参差荇菜，左右芼之。窈窕淑女，钟鼓乐之。
